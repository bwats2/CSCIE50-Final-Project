What is this site?

This site is a lightweight web app designed to help users who might struggle with mental health and wellbeing.


How does this site attempt to help?

Our methodology is fundamentally based in the Cognitive Distortions Theory of mental health. Most crisis is caused by a cognitive distortion, and being able to rationally think through distortions can relieve the burden they place on the indvidual.


What is a Cognitive Distortion?

Please click here to learn more about the different types of Cognitive Distortions.


What is a 'Support Contact'?

A Support Contact - created during the registration process - is a contact who you can reach out to in times of need. This could be a family member, friend, therapist, etc. Research has shown that it can be hard to initiate a request for help from your support network, so we try to reduce the perceived/real barriers to asking for support.
